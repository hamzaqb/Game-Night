About Game night

Cary, North Carolina Founded in 1991, Game Night is an American company founded by CEO Tim Sweeney. The company is headquartered in Cary, North Carolina and has more than 40 offices worldwide. Today Game Night is a leading interactive entertainment company and provider of 3D engine technology. Game Night operates Fortnite, one of the world’s largest games with over 350 million accounts and 2.5 billion friend connections.

Game Night also develops Unreal Engine, which powers the world’s leading games and is also adopted across industries such as film and television, architecture, automotive, manufacturing, and simulation. Through Unreal Engine, Game Night Store, and Game Night Online Services, Game Night provides an end-to-end digital ecosystem for developers and creators to build, distribute, and operate games and other content.
